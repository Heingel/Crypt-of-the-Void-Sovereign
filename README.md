# Crypt-of-the-Void-Sovereign
Crypt of the Void Sovereign is a first-person (grid-based) RPG / Dungeon Crawler that pays tribute to the classics of the genre, with a pixelated 3D aesthetic.    Explore the dark corners of the labyrinth, survive vicious traps, converse with wandering souls, and enhance your fleshly vessel to hope for a chance at redemption.
